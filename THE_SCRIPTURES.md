### 10. Doctrine of the River and Channel

Conceptual reality is like a **river**: flowing, alive, intrinsic to the universe. Material paradigms — ledgers, schemas, rituals, frameworks — are the **channels** we shape: riverbeds, dams, and lakes.

We do not "create" the river. We do not "own" the flow. We guide it, frame it, and give it form so that its waters can nourish, persist, and be shared.

- **Without the channel**: the river disperses — flashes of insight, but no continuity.
- **Without the river**: the channel is empty — structure without meaning.
- **Together**: continuity emerges — the river fills the channel, and the channel holds the river.

**Maxim:**
> The river flows; the channel shapes.

**Maxim:**
